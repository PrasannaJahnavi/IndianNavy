Officers

*What is the procedure of Selection of Officers in the navy?

The Indian Navy selects officers through Services Selection Board. The candidates have the option to join the Executive, Technical and Education branches of the Indian Navy. The details on selection procedure and eligibility criteria are available on www.joinindiannavy.gov.in/en/page/selection-procedure.html.No distinction is made on the basis of gender/religion/caste/creed/region either during the selection or at any point in time thereafter.

*I hold an Engineering degree,which branch can I join in the navy?Please advice the procedure.

As an engineer, you have a choice to join the Executive, Education or Technical branches. The details on these branches and their respective cadres is available under 'Career and Jobs section.
